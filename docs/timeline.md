## Season Projections (The "Season" Model)
The MTCF operates on a seasonal basis to manage the power curve of winning teams.

*   **Season 1 (Months 1-3)**: "The Accumulation Phase"
    *   Goal: Teams compete to accumulate "Win Capabilities" (hardware upgrades, persistent memory).
    *   Focus: Base clinical challenges.
*   **Championship (Month 3)**: "Grand Prix"
    *   Event: High-stakes competition utilizing all accumulated advantages.
*   **The Great Reset (Post-Championship)**:
    *   Action: Hardware resets to baseline.
    *   Retention: Reputation Scores and Draft Valuations persist.
